The components can be split in three different rolls:

- **Audio**
  - 2 Shure MX 393/C
  - 1 Focusrite Scarlett (4th Gen) 2i2 Audio Interface
  - 2 Mini XLR to XLR Adapters
  - 1 USB type C to type A cable
- **Video**
  - 2 USB PTZ Cameras
  - 1 Logitech Brio 4K Web Cam
  - 2 USB 2.0 Micro B to type A cables
- **Recording**
  - 1 Omen Laptop
  - OBS
